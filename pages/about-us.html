About Us

AusGSTPro was built to make Australian GST calculations simple, fast, and trustworthy for everyday users and growing businesses.

Last updated: May 2026

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide practical, accurate, and no-noise tax calculation tools for Australians. We focus on clarity, speed, and usability so users can get the right GST result without complicated workflows.

Why We Built AusGSTPro

Many online calculators are cluttered, ad-heavy, or difficult to use on mobile. We wanted to ship a cleaner experience with clear UI states, reliable formulas, and content that helps users understand what each number means.

Who We Build For

AusGSTPro is used by sole traders, small businesses, contractors, bookkeepers, and individuals who need quick GST checks. We design for real-world tasks like quoting jobs, checking invoices, and understanding GST-inclusive values.

Our Product Principles

  • Accuracy first: formulas and outputs are deterministic and transparent.
  • User-first design: mobile-friendly layouts and accessible interactions.
  • Clear communication: concise labels, meaningful validation, and plain language.
  • Trust and privacy: minimal data collection and straightforward policies.

What We Are Improving Next

We continue refining accessibility, performance, legal clarity, and SEO structure to keep the site useful and production-ready. User feedback directly informs our roadmap and priorities.
