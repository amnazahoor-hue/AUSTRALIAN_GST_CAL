AusGSTPro is a small, independent project built by Australians for Australians. We read every message that comes through and aim to reply within 24 to 48 hours during business days. There is no automated bot answering our inbox — when you write to us, a real person reads what you've sent.

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 to 48 hours during Australian business days (Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm AEST). Messages received on weekends or public holidays are usually answered on the next business day.

What to Include

To help us help you faster, please include the following details with your message where they're relevant:

  • A clear description of the issue, question, or feedback.
  • The device and browser you were using (for example, "iPhone 14, Safari").
  • Any error messages, screenshots, or screen recordings that show the problem.
  • The steps you took just before the issue happened, so we can reproduce it.

The more context you give us, the faster we can get to the bottom of it. We treat all information you share with us in line with our Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Many enquiries are already answered on our home page. Common questions include:

  • How do I calculate 10% GST? Multiply the base amount by 1.1 to get the GST-inclusive total.
  • How do I remove GST from an inclusive price? Divide the inclusive price by 11 to find the GST portion, or by 1.1 to find the base amount.
  • What is the current GST rate in Australia? The standard rate is 10% and has been since July 2000.

For more, see our full FAQ section on the home page.

A Note on Professional Advice

AusGSTPro is a free utility and educational resource. We cannot provide personalised tax, accounting, or legal advice — and any response we give to a support enquiry is general information only. For specific tax decisions, please consult a registered tax agent or qualified accountant. You can also visit ato.gov.au for official guidance from the Australian Taxation Office.

Feedback & Feature Requests

We are constantly improving AusGSTPro based on the feedback we receive from real users. If there's a feature you'd love to see — additional GST scenarios, support for other tax types, an offline mode, a printable invoice template, or anything else — we genuinely want to hear about it. Your suggestion may well shape the next update of the calculator.

When sending feature requests, please tell us a little about how you'd use it. Knowing whether you're a sole trader, a bookkeeper handling multiple clients, a tradesperson invoicing on-site, or someone managing personal finances helps us prioritise what to build next.

Reporting a Bug

If something on the site isn't working the way you expect, please let us know. Bugs are best reported by email with as much detail as possible. Helpful information includes the exact values you typed into the calculator, the result it showed, the result you expected, and the device you were using. If the issue is visual — for example, something not displaying correctly — a screenshot makes a huge difference.

We treat bug reports as a priority and do our best to push fixes quickly. Thank you in advance for helping us make AusGSTPro better for everyone.

Privacy of Your Enquiry

Any information you send us through the contact form or by email is handled in line with our Privacy Policy. We use your details only to respond to your message, never to send unsolicited marketing, and never share them with third parties for promotional purposes. You can request that we delete your correspondence at any time by replying to your support email thread.

Why We Built AusGSTPro

AusGSTPro started as a side project to scratch our own itch. Existing GST calculators on the web felt slow, cluttered, full of adverts, or simply incorrect on edge cases. We thought Australians deserved a clean, fast, accurate, and free tool that respects their time and privacy. Every detail of this site — from the calculator's instant response, to the absence of trackers, to the considered typography — has been designed with that goal in mind. Your feedback keeps that mission honest.
